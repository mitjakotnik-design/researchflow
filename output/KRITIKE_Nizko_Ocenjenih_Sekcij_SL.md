### ❌ KRITIČNE TEŽAVE


#### 🟠 VEČJE TEŽAVE (visoka prioriteta)


**1. Insufficient justification for scoping review methodology.** (Metodolog)

- **Kategorija:** Methodology
- **Lokacija:** Paragraph 2, and implicitly throughout the aims in Paragraph 3.
- **Vpliv:** Without a clear rationale for choosing a scoping review, readers (and reviewers) may question the methodological appropriateness. PRISMA-ScR emphasizes a clear rationale for the review type.
- **Dokaz:** "The text states 'This scoping review aims to systematically map...' but doesn't explain *why* a scoping review is the most suitable approach for the identified gaps, as opposed to a systematic review or other synthesis methods."


**2. Lack of theoretical grounding and specific literature engagement.** (Strokovnjak področja)

- **Kategorija:** Content
- **Lokacija:** Paragraph 2: 'Existing literature has explored AI applications... However, these studies often present a fragmented view...'
- **Vpliv:** The absence of specific theoretical frameworks or engagement with key debates in HR/AI literature weakens the academic contribution. The 'fragmented view' claim feels unsubstantiated without specific examples.
- **Dokaz:** "The statement 'Existing literature has explored AI applications in specific HR functions... However, these studies often present a fragmented view...' is too general. It doesn't mention any specific theories (e.g., socio-technical systems, resource-based view, ethical AI frameworks) or specific authors/studies to illustrate the fragmentation."


**3. Vague articulation of practical relevance and actionable insights.** (Praktik)

- **Kategorija:** Contribution
- **Lokacija:** Paragraph 3: 'By providing a comprehensive overview, this review will serve as a foundational resource for academics, practitioners, and policymakers...'
- **Vpliv:** Practitioners need to understand the direct utility and actionable takeaways from the research. A general statement about being a 'foundational resource' is insufficient to convey concrete value.
- **Dokaz:** "The statement 'this review will serve as a foundational resource for academics, practitioners, and policymakers navigating the evolving landscape of AI in HR' is broad. It doesn't specify *how* practitioners can use this resource to make decisions, address challenges, or implement AI more effectively."

### 💡 PRIPOROČILA ZA IZBOLJŠAVE


#### 🎯 VISOKA PRIORITETA


**1. Methodological justification** (Metodolog) — *+5 points*

**Trenutni problem:**  
The rationale for choosing a scoping review over other review types is not explicitly stated.

**Priporočena rešitev:**  
Add a sentence or two explaining why a scoping review is the most appropriate methodology for the identified research question and the current state of the literature (e.g., to map emerging concepts, identify research gaps in a broad field, or explore the nature of evidence).

**Primer:**  
Given the nascent and fragmented nature of research on AI in HRM, a scoping review is particularly suited to systematically map the breadth of existing evidence, identify key concepts, and delineate areas requiring further investigation, rather than synthesizing findings from a narrow, well-defined question.


**2. Theoretical grounding and specific literature engagement** (Strokovnjak) — *+7 points*

**Trenutni problem:**  
The discussion of existing literature and gaps is generic, lacking specific examples or theoretical context.

**Priporočena rešitev:**  
Briefly mention one or two relevant theoretical lenses (e.g., socio-technical systems theory, ethical AI frameworks) that underpin the HR/AI intersection. Provide specific examples of existing fragmented studies to strengthen the gap argument.

**Primer:**  
While studies have explored AI's role in specific HR functions, often through a technological determinism lens (e.g., Smith, 2020 on AI recruitment; Jones, 2021 on predictive analytics), there is a critical need to understand these applications through a broader socio-technical perspective, mapping their integration across the entire HR lifecycle and considering emerging ethical dilemmas (e.g., algorithmic bias, data privacy) that current literature addresses in isolation.


**3. Articulation of practical relevance and actionable insights** (Praktik) — *+5 points*

**Trenutni problem:**  
The practical contribution is vaguely stated as being a 'foundational resource.'

**Priporočena rešitev:**  
Explicitly state how the review's findings will be useful for practitioners. Detail the types of actionable insights they can expect (e.g., best practices, challenges to anticipate, frameworks for implementation, future skill requirements).

**Primer:**  
Beyond mapping the landscape, this review will offer practitioners actionable insights into effective AI implementation strategies, identify common pitfalls, highlight critical ethical considerations for HR decision-making, and inform the development of future HR competencies required to leverage AI responsibly.


#### 🎯 SREDNJA PRIORITETA


**1. Precision and conciseness of language** (academic_editor) — *+3 points*

**Trenutni problem:**  
The introduction uses several generic phrases that could be more precise and impactful.

**Priporočena rešitev:**

**Primer:**
